Couscous

Description

This is a quick and easy way to make something delicious. Whether you are working frequently and don't have the time or you just detest cooking, this dish has you covered. It is based off a traditional Moroccan cuisine whereby succulent pieces of meat and steamed vegetables are added to the couscous followed by an almost soup-like broth.

We have decided to focus on making the couscous portion of the dish. This means that you can add to and flavour the dish however you like! For instance, you could add meat, chicken and whatever vegetables you desire to enhance the flavour.

Ingredients

  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Fresh Garlic
  • Chicken Broth
  • Lemon
  • Salt
  • Parsley
  • Moroccan couscous

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a medium saucepan over medium-low heat.
  2. Add garlic and saute until fragrant (not toasting and browning or it will be bitter!), about 20 seconds.
  3. Remove from heat, pour in chicken broth, lemon zest, lemon juice and season with salt to taste. Place over medium-high heat and bring to a boil.
  4. Pour in couscous, stir then remove from heat and immediately cover with lid. Let rest 4 minutes off heat.
  5. Add parsley and fluff with a fork. Serve warm.
