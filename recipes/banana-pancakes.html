Banana Pancakes

Description

Whether you're looking for a quick little brunch treat or craving something sweet, Banana Pancakes are extremely easy to make. With just 3 ingredients this recipe should take a few minutes and you'll be ready to enjoy in no-time!

The easiness of this recipe makes it ideal for anyone and everyone.

Ingredients

  • 4 Overripe bananas
  • 4 Eggs
  • 1 cup of All-purpose flour
  • 8 teaspoons of Butter (divided)
  • Optional toppings of choice

Steps

  1. Add the bananas to a bowl and mash with a fork until there are no chunks. Add the eggs and flour to the bowl and mix until combined.
  2. Melt two teaspoons of butter in non-stick pan over medium heat. Add quarter of pancake batter and spread to create circular shape.
  3. Cook for 2 mins and flip. If the bottom is still sticking to pan, leave for an extra minute or so until it is easily able to flip. Repeat for other side of pancake.
  4. Garnish with favourite toppings and serve.
