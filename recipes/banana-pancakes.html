Banana Pancakes

Description

Whether you're looking for a quick little brunch treat or craving something sweet, Banana Pancakes are extremely easy to make. With just 3 ingredients this recipe should take a few minutes and you'll be ready to enjoy in no-time!

The easiness of this recipe makes it ideal for anyone and everyone.

Ingredients

  • 4 Overripe bananas
  • 4 Eggs
  • 1 cup of All-purpose flour
  • 8 teaspoons of Butter (divided)
  • Optional toppings of choice
