Chocolate Fudge Cake

Description

Fancy a dessert?Look no further. For all the chocolate lovers out there, this delicious, yet simple recipe will satisfy your tastebuds. A classic. Chocolate Fudge Cake.

  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder

  • 150g caster sugar
  • 2 tbsp golden syrup
  • 2 large eggs, lightly beaten
  • 150ml semi-skimmed milk
